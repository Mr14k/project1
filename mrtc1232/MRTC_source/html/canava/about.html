Company overview

Maa Rudra Transport: The Logistics Company That Puts Your Needs First Maa Rudra Transport Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 2018 by Subodh Kumar with a big dream and motive: to provide the best possible logistics services to businesses of all sizes. In just five years, Maa Rudra Transport has grown to become one of the leading logistics companies in India. The company operates in 10 states across the country and has a fleet of over 100 trucks. Maa Rudra Transport's success is due to its commitment to providing excellent customer service. The company's team of experienced professionals is dedicated to ensuring that your shipments are delivered on time and in perfect condition. Maa Rudra Transport also offers a wide range of value-added services, such as: Packaging and labeling Order fulfillment Customs clearance Tracking and tracing With Maa Rudra Transport, you can be confident that your shipments will be handled with care and delivered on time. The company is committed to providing its customers with the best possible service, and is confident that it can help you achieve your business goals. Call to action: Contact Maa Rudra Transport today to learn more about how the company can help you with your logistics needs.

We will take care of your cargo or your passenger and deliver them safe and on time.

At Maa Rudra Transport, we understand that safety is our top priority. We are committed to providing our customers, employees, and communities with the safest possible transportation experience. We understand the importance of continuous training, and we are proud of our safety knowledge, experienced staff, and ability to exceed industry standards year after year. We have established and continually maintain excellent motor carrier safety ratings and low accident frequencies.

We are confident that our commitment to safety makes us the best choice for your transportation needs. When you choose Maa Rudra Transport, you can be sure that your shipments will be handled with the utmost care and attention to safety.

Maa Rudra Transport understands that your assets are important to you. That's why we offer industry-leading asset protection and security compliance programs. We work with you to understand your specific needs and develop a customized solution that meets your requirements.

We leverage modern and proven technologies to protect your assets while in transit or at one of our facilities. We also offer consultation and proactive partnership to ensure that your security needs are met.
