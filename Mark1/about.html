Company overview

We began our journey in the field of logistics & transportation as Maa Rudra Transport Carriers Pvt. Ltd. known as MRTC in the year 2018 under the leadership of honourable founder Mr. Subodh Kumar. Later, we got registered as Pvt Ltd. In April 2021 Although we are a new firm with a very promising start. We are a team of experienced and skilful workers who have been hand-picked by Mr Subodh Kumar. Our Team constitutes veterans as well as youths. We are a team of agile, open-minded, enthusiastic and innovative people, in whom we take pride We are single step solution for all type of goods. we work aroud the tagline - "We, deliver not only the goods, but also trust and happiness with it"

    • OUR EXPANSION:-
    • Our USP

    Since our inception, we have been focused and very particular about our expansion, we started with 1 booking and 3 delivery branches. today, we cover more of the 20 districts in Bihar. not limited to Bihar only, we have also covered Gujarat, Haryana, Maharashtra, Rajasthan, Delhi, Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh. Our upcoming projects are pipelined for southern parts of India too. We as a team, are primarily focused on quality rather than quantity. Our expansion may seem slow, but whatever we cover, we earn mastery of that geographical location

    We are confident that our commitment to safety makes us the best choice for your transportation needs. When you choose Maa Rudra Transport, you can be sure that your shipments will be handled with the utmost care and attention to safety.

    Our Quality services are a derived result of effective collaboration among the skilled and experienced team, technology and innovative ideas we gave a real-time advanced GPS tracking system which makes tracking goods at the fingertips. We have a unique billing and ERP system which smoothens our workflow as well as provides a hassle-free experience to our customers. We have special care for customers’ needs. We are cost-effective as well as competitive in providing faster and more secured service
